About

Everest Trail is a multiplayer voxel adventure set in the Himalayas. Trek through dramatic terrain, manage altitude and endurance, and follow saga-driven narratives as you ascend toward the summit.

Built on the Ambient engine with real elevation data, dynamic climate, and interactive characters along the trail.
